Welcome Casa Maranta
Welcome!

We are Christine and Markus, together with our daughters Nora and Olivia, and we are delighted to welcome you to our holiday home.

This house has been in the Maranta family for generations. Christine's father was born here and spent his childhood and youth within these walls before leaving for Zurich in his twenties. In the 1970s, after the passing of his parents, he took over the house and turned it into a family holiday home.

For Christine, Poschiavo has always been a place full of special memories, shaped by many happy family holidays. In 2013, we were fortunate to take over the house ourselves and carefully renovate it with great care and love. Our connection with Poschiavo – its nature, its people and its wonderful food – has grown steadily ever since.

In this guide you will find not only all the important information about the house, but also our personal highlights: favourite restaurants, excursion ideas, places to explore, and tips for shopping and enjoying the region.

Warm regards,
Christine & Markus

About the House Casa Maranta
About the House

We have furnished our holiday home with great care to make it as cosy, comfortable and functional as possible – for us and for our guests. Our goal is that you feel just as at home here as we do ourselves.

On the following pages you will find helpful information about the house – from using the facilities to practical tips for everyday life.

🔥 Wood-Burning Stove in the Living Room

For us, ending the evening by a crackling fire is one of the most special moments in this holiday home. To help you use the stove safely and easily, please follow these guidelines:

Safety Rules

  • Make sure the flue is open: the lever must be vertical
  • Always keep the door closed while in use
  • Do not place anything on top of the stove
  • Always let ash cool completely before putting it in the bucket
  • If in doubt, contact Anna (+41 79 422 16 08)

After Use

  • Dispose of cooled ash in the bucket next to the stove
  • Clean the glass if needed

🎭 Decorative Stove in the Attic

⚠️ IMPORTANT: The stove in the attic is purely decorative – it is not connected and must not be used.
Kitchen Appliances Casa Maranta
Kitchen Appliances

🍳 Hob / Cooktop

Switching On

  • Switch the appliance on (1)
  • Select the cooking zone (2)
  • Select the desired temperature in the control area (3)

Switching Off a Zone

  • Select the cooking zone (2)
  • Tap 0 in the control area (4)

Child Lock

The hob must be switched off first.

  • Activate: Touch the key symbol (5) for approx. 4 seconds. The indicator light glows for 10 seconds – hob is locked.
  • Deactivate: Touch the key symbol (5) again for ~4 seconds.

🍽 Dishwasher

⚠️ Please do not put Teflon-coated pans and pots in the dishwasher.

Dishwasher tabs, rinse aid and salt: behind the bin.

We recommend the Eco programme. Use Intensive only for heavily soiled loads.

Switching On

  • Switch the appliance on (1)
  • Select the Eco programme (2)
  • Press Start (3)

Note: During the programme a red light dot appears on the floor beneath the door. Only open the door once the light dot is no longer visible.

Doors & Windows Casa Maranta
Doors & Windows

🚪 Front Door

The front door is fitted with a Nuki Go smart lock and an external keypad. Use your personal access code to open the door.

The door locks automatically every evening at 11:00 pm. Please close the door fully after entering.

🪟 Skylight Windows

The skylight windows in the attic can be opened and closed using a pole – you'll find it behind the door in the attic bedroom. Please always close the windows in bad weather and when leaving the house.

Doors marked "Privat" on the ground floor and in the attic lead to cellar, utility and private rooms – these are not accessible to guests.

Approx. 180 m towards Li Curt. In the municipality of Poschiavo, household waste is disposed of at central collection points – unfortunately there are no bins directly at the house.

Shopping & Activities Casa Maranta
Activities & Excursions
  • 💎 Lago di Saoseo
    Crystal-blue mountain lake in the Val da Camp nature reserve. Hike from Sfazù or take the small PostBus (limited seats, book in advance!).
  • 🧊 Cavaglia Glacier Garden
    Impressive giant's pots and a gorge. From Cavaglia station on the Rhaetian Railway (UNESCO World Heritage). New gorge path added in 2021.
  • ⛳ Minigolf Le Prese
    18 holes next to Hotel La Romantica · perfect for families and friends · cash only.
  • 🗿 Legend Trail (San Piero)
    Sculpture trail through old larches. Approx. 2 km, no particular difficulty. Suitable for all ages.
  • ⛪ Chiesa di San Romerio
    Medieval church on a rocky ledge high above Lake Poschiavo · magnificent views.
  • 🏔 Diavolezza (Bernina)
    Spectacular mountain world · glacier, hiking, via ferratas · Piz Palü & Piz Bernina.
