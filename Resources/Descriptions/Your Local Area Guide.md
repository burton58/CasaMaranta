**Activities**

So many things to see
There is so much to see and experience in the region, and we would like to share a few of our personal highlights that we especially enjoy. For many more ideas and activities, you can also visit Valposchiavo Tourism Office or explore their website at valposchiavo.ch/de/explore – there is truly something for everyone!

Lago di Saoseo Pic:https://media.myswitzerland.com/image/fetch/c_lfill,g_auto,w_3200,h_1800/f_auto,q_80,fl_keep_iptc/https://www.myswitzerland.com/-/media/st/gadmin/images/web-team%20only/images%20from%20id%2075/sth5750h_45930.jpg GPS: 46.398855887937344, 10.126257298365717
The Lagh da Saoseo is absolutely one of the most stunning destinations we know — a true highlight of the region. Surrounded by larches, stone pines, and dramatic alpine scenery, the intense blue waters of the lake create a breathtaking and unforgettable setting that we love to visit again and again.

Reaching the lake is part of the adventure. You can hike through the peaceful Val da Camp nature reserve from Sfazù, but you can also use the local PostAuto / Postbus service. From the PostBus stop at Sfazù, a small bus takes you up to Lungacqua or Alp Camp — but seats are limited and you need to book in advance to secure a place on the bus.


The Cavaglia Glacier Garden Pic:https://ggc.swiss/images/orrido/passerella-orrido.jpg GPS: 46.36029945734483, 10.048121868065586
The Cavaglia Glacier Garden is one of our absolute favourite places in the region. Shaped by ancient glaciers, this remarkable natural site is known for its impressive giants’ pots, alpine vegetation, and surprisingly diverse flora. Walking through the garden feels like stepping into a landscape sculpted by time.

Located in Valposchiavo, the Glacier Garden is easily reached from Cavaglia station on the famous Rhaetian Railway Red Train, a UNESCO World Heritage route. Since 2021, a new path along the Cavaglia Gorge reveals even more fascinating rock formations and erosion-shaped landscapes.

A truly special place that we enjoy visiting again and again, and one we highly recommend to our guests.


Minigolf Le Prese Pic:https://www.datocms-assets.com/118512/1740061856-minigolf_ok.tiff?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&q=75&w=1920&auto=compress&fm=avif&ar=800%3A1000&fp-x=0.50000000&fp-y=0.50000000 GPS: 46.29190618820981, 10.079156647875372
We love coming to Minigolf Le Prese with our daughters and friends. Located right next to Albergo La Romantica, this charming 18-hole minigolf course is perfect for relaxed family time — and for a bit of healthy, good-natured competition among friends.

Set in the green garden of Grosi Rosemarie and surrounded by colourful flowers and lush plants, it offers a warm and inviting atmosphere where children and adults alike can enjoy the fresh air, have fun together, and celebrate small victories along the way.

Please note: payment is cash only.



Legend Trail Pic:https://media-v2.discover.swiss/image/img_4kq_gaebfiie?nobiblio=true&width=1050&height=560 GPS: 46.32589281436307, 10.053430150862265
The Legend Trail starts near the church of San Piero and winds through old larches, firs, and colorful deciduous trees. It is an artistic route suitable for everyone, including families and school groups. Just under 2 km long, it does not present particular difficulties.

On the occasion of the Valposchiavo Expo 2018, several artists and sculptors created statues representing various characters from epic poems. The trail begins with a knight on horseback about to fall into a cliff, signed by the local sculptor Angelo Boninchi. The trail continues narrow and steep, reaching a sequence of wooden sculptures, carved with chainsaws directly in the trees. Among the wood sculptors are the artists Arianna Gasperina from Valvasone (PN) and Nicola Cozzio (Val Rendena). At the end of the route, you reach one of the most beautiful panoramic points in the valley.
